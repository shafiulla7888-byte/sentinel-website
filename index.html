A Silent Guardian for Your Child's Digital Well-being.

Sentinel is a non-intrusive, AI-powered companion that acts as a compassionate safety net, guiding your child toward better mental health in the digital age.

The Sentinel Solution

Privacy by Design

Our AI analyzes metadata and public activity, not private conversations. Your child’s privacy is our top priority.

Proactive Insights

We detect subtle shifts in digital behavior to identify potential emotional distress before it escalates.

Compassionate Intervention

Instead of an alarm, Sentinel offers a gentle, private prompt and one-click access to vetted resources.

How Sentinel Works

1. Silent Monitoring

Sentinel works seamlessly in the background. It’s an invisible layer, a silent guardian that doesn't demand engagement or attention from your child.

2. Dilemma Detection

Our proprietary AI analyzes high-level behavioral patterns—not message content—to spot emotional distress. We look for a change in sentiment, screen time patterns, or communication style.

3. Gentle Prompt

When a pattern is detected, a private, non-alarming message appears, like "Hey, I'm here if you want to talk." It's an invitation, not an alert.

4. One-Click Support

If the child chooses to engage, they get immediate access to a curated list of vetted, age-appropriate resources—from crisis hotlines to guided meditations and educational content.
